About me

Bachelor's degree and Professor in Visual Arts
(National University of the Arts of Argentina -2015/2023-).
I have participated in exhibitions in both galleries and public art institutions, including Galería Pasaje17, Ex-Esma (Espacio Cuatro Columnas), Contemporary Arts Exhibition Centre Casona de los Olivera, Jardín Botánico Carlos Thays and Quinta El Ombú, San Fernando's Art Exhibition Centre.
In 2015, the National Salon of Visual Arts selected my work "Body Tissues" in the Textile Art category.
