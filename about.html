BIO

Bachelor's Degree and Professor in Visual Arts (U.N.A 2015). She studied painting in Alejandro Avakian's workshop and participated in work clinics with Gabriel Baggio. She has been involved in exhibitions in both galleries and public art institutions, including Galería Pasaje17, Ex-Esma (Espacio Cuatro Columnas), Centro de Exposición de Arte Contemporáneo Casona de los Olivera, Jardín Botánico Carlos Thays, Quinta El Ombú, and Espacio de Arte Contemporáneo de San Fernando. In 2015, the Salón Nacional selected a work by the artist in the "Textile Art" category.
